Kelly Franklin Music Group (KFMG) is an Artist-Centered Record Label founded in Oakland, CA by Les Franklin and Clinton Kelly.  We are staffed by songwriters, musicians, and others devoted to working with our fellow artists and helping each other grow.  We want to help empower you by providing informational resources and constructive feedback while handling the tedium of paperwork so you can focus on your art.
